# Getting Started

Setting up on Premarket takes a few steps before you can place your first trade. You will need to verify your identity, create a smart account, and deposit funds. This page walks you through each step in order.

> Screenshot: Premarket app homepage

## Step 1: Access the App

Go to [app.premarket.xyz](https://app.premarket.xyz) and navigate to the Markets section. You can browse active markets without any setup, but placing a trade requires completing the steps below.

## Step 2: Complete KYC Verification

> Screenshot: Stripe Identity verification screen

To trade on prediction markets you must verify your identity. Click **Buy Yes** or **Buy No** on any prediction market and you will be redirected to verification via Stripe Identity. Submit a government issued ID and complete face verification if prompted. Once done, return to the app and your account will be enabled for trading.

## Step 3: Create Your Smart Account

Premarket uses a smart account as your trading wallet. Go to the Wallet page, click **Create Smart Account**, and wait for confirmation. All your funds, trades, and positions are managed through this account.

> Screenshot: Smart account creation screen

## Step 4: Deposit Funds

> Screenshot: Deposit screen showing token selector and balance

Once your smart account is created, click **Deposit** and send supported assets to your smart account. Wait for your balance to update before placing a trade. Make sure you are depositing on the correct network.

## Step 5: Place Your First Trade

> Screenshot: Trade panel with outcome selected and amount entered

Go to any active market and select an outcome. For prediction markets choose **Buy Yes** or **Buy No**. For FDV band markets select the band you want to trade and mint your position. Enter an amount, review the price, shares, and potential payout, then click **Buy** to execute.

## Step 6: Manage Your Position

> Screenshot: Portfolio page showing open position

After placing a trade, track it in your Portfolio. You can monitor price changes and exit early by selling your position before settlement, provided there is liquidity available on the other side.

## Common Issues

If you completed KYC but still cannot trade, you likely need to create your smart account or deposit funds first. Work through the steps above in order and both issues will resolve themselves.

If the issue persists, reach out to the official Premarket team on [Telegram](https://t.me/premarket) for support.

For a detailed walkthrough of the KYC process, head to How to Complete KYC on Premarket.
